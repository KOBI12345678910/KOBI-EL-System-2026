## 1. Purpose

Generate the Israeli annual personal income-tax return (**טופס 1301 — דוח שנתי ליחיד**) for the 2026 tax year. The engine:

1. Aggregates every income category (employment, self-employed, capital, rental, pension, other).
2. Applies allowed deductions (pension, study-fund, life, donation).
3. Computes progressive bracket tax using the 2026 ladder (10/14/20/31/35/47/50%).
4. Adds 3% יסף (high-earner surtax) on income > 698,280 NIS per § 121ב.
5. Subtracts credit points (נקודות זיכוי × 2,976 NIS) and named credits (residence, shift-work).
6. Handles רגילים ופטורים (taxable / exempt splits) + spouse (separate-computation joint filing).
7. Reconciles against `withholding` to produce final balance (positive = balance due; negative = refund).
8. Emits a section-by-section `fields` map matching the official Form 1301 layout (line numbers + bilingual labels).

Effective top marginal rate at the 7th bracket: **50%** (47% bracket + 3% surtax baked into the 50% step). The surtax is *also* reported as its own line (`section_06_computation.surtax`) so auditors can see it on a dedicated row.

## 7. Married Filing Logic

- Per `normalizeInput`, an optional `spouse` branch is accepted at the top level (`input.spouse`).
- **Separate-computation** (חישוב נפרד) is applied: each spouse's taxable-income ladder is computed independently; credit-points are aggregated separately; the two `computedTax` totals are summed.
- Joint filing (חישוב מאוחד) is **not** the default — per מדינת ישראל, separate is mandatory when incomes are independent; joint is only used on request and is a future enhancement (stubbed via `createEngine` options).
- Joint withholding is summed and reconciled against the combined `netTax` to produce the final balance.

## 9. Validation Notes & Edge Cases

- **Israeli ID validation**: uses the standard Luhn-like mod-10 check; invalid IDs do **not** throw — they are recorded in `diagnostics.validationErrors` so partial returns still render.
- **Required**: only `taxpayer.id` — every other field defaults safely.
- **Negative inputs**: coerced by `normalizeInput`; all amounts floor at zero after exempt subtraction (`nn()`).
- **NaN / null / undefined**: universally coerced to 0.
- **Rental tracks**:
  - `normal` — included in bracket ladder (gross − expenses − exempt).
  - `10pct` — excluded from ladder; taxed at flat 10%; shown as exempt in section 3.
  - `exempt` — excluded entirely.
- **Capital income**: taxed at flat 25% (simplified individual rate); included in the § 121ב surtax base.
- **Donation cap**: 30% of non-capital taxable; `diagnostics.deductionNotes` emits `donation_capped_30pct` when triggered.
